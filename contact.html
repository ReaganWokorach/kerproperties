Common Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often.

You will need a valid national ID or passport. We handle all the transfer forms and guide you through the Lands Registry process. For mailo or freehold titles, the transfer is registered at the relevant Lands Office on your behalf.
Our commission is transparent and agreed upfront before any transaction. For land sales it is typically a small percentage of the sale price paid by the seller. For rentals, one month's rent is the standard agency fee, paid once on lease signing.
Yes — we operate across all districts of Uganda. From Kampala, Wakiso, and Entebbe in the Central Region to Mbarara, Kabale, and Kasese in the West; Mbale, Jinja, and Tororo in the East; and Gulu, Lira, Arua, and more in the North. Contact us for any district.
