Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Serving All of Uganda

Ker Properties was founded with one mission: to make property ownership and quality housing accessible to every Ugandan family — regardless of where they live. What started as a small brokerage has grown into one of Uganda's most recognised real estate names, with active listings in over 50 districts.

We specialise in verified land sales — freehold, mailo, leasehold, and customary titles — and connect tenants with well-maintained rental homes from Kampala to Gulu, Mbarara to Mbale, and everywhere in between. Every listing on our platform is personally verified by our team before it goes live.

Our clients trust us because we are transparent about prices, honest about title status, and always available to answer questions — in person, by phone, or on WhatsApp.

What We Stand For

Everything we do is guided by these core principles.

Verified Listings

Every property is physically inspected and title documents confirmed before listing. No guesswork, no surprises.

Full Transparency

Fast Turnaround

From first enquiry to signed agreement, we move quickly so you do not lose a great property to someone else.

Nationwide Expertise

We know Uganda's property landscape deeply — from Kampala's suburbs to Northern Uganda's growing towns and beyond.

Fair Dealing
