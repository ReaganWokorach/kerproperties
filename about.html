Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Rooted in Gulu

Prestige Properties was founded in Gulu City with one mission: to make property ownership and quality housing accessible to every Ugandan family. What started as a small land brokerage has grown into one of Northern Uganda's most recognised real estate names.

We specialise in verified land sales — freehold, mailo, and customary titles — and connect tenants with well-maintained rental homes across Gulu, Kampala, Lira, and beyond. Every listing on our platform is personally verified by our team before it goes live.

Our clients trust us because we are transparent about prices, honest about title status, and always available to answer questions — in person, by phone, or on WhatsApp.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Everything we do is guided by these core principles.

Verified Listings

Every property is physically inspected and title documents confirmed before being listed. No guesswork, no surprises.

Full Transparency

We tell you exactly what you are buying or renting — price, title type, boundaries, and any encumbrances — upfront.

Client First

Our team works around your schedule. Site visits, documentation help, and follow-ups are all part of the service — at no extra cost.

Fast Turnaround

From first enquiry to signed agreement, we move quickly so you do not lose a great property to someone else.

Local Expertise

We know Gulu and Northern Uganda deeply — which divisions are growing, where infrastructure is headed, and what fair market prices look like.

Fair Dealing

We negotiate on your behalf and never inflate prices. Our commission is earned through service, not shortcuts.
